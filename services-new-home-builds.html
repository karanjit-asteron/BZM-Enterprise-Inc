01 —The Approach

Right-sized homes,
built deliberately.

BZM specializes in custom homes up to 2,000 square feet — a deliberate ceiling that lets us stay close to every detail without diluting our attention across mega-builds.

Whether you're building on an Edmonton infill lot, putting up a garden suite for family, or starting from scratch with a custom plan — the same crew is on your project from foundation to final walkthrough. No subcontractor shuffling, no telephone games, no surprises in the final invoice.

Every BZM build is engineered to current Alberta Building Code, fully permitted, and backed by a written workmanship warranty. The result: a home that's right-sized for the way you actually live.

04 —The Timeline

Five phases,
one build.

A typical BZM small home runs six to nine months from contract to keys. Here's what each phase looks like — and how long it should take.

Phase 01

Design & Permitting

Drawings, engineering, finish selections, and full permit submission to the City of Edmonton. We walk you through every decision before construction starts.

Duration6 – 10 weeks
Phase 02

Site Prep & Foundation

Excavation, grading, footings, foundation walls, and weeping tile. Engineered, inspected, and signed off before framing begins.

Duration3 – 4 weeks
Phase 03

Framing & Lock-Up

Floor systems, walls, roof structure, sheathing, windows, exterior doors. The home goes weather-tight before interior work starts.

Duration4 – 6 weeks

Mechanical & Finishes

Rough-in plumbing, electrical, HVAC, insulation, drywall, paint, flooring, cabinets, millwork, tile, fixtures. The longest phase — and the most rewarding.

Duration10 – 14 weeks

Final Inspection & Hand-Off

Punch list, deficiency walk, final municipal inspection, occupancy permit, and key hand-off — with a written warranty in your hand.

Duration1 – 2 weeks
05 —Common Questions

What people
ask before they build.

— 01 Why do you cap builds at 2,000 sq/ft?
It's a deliberate choice. At our size, capping square footage means we can keep one team on every project, stay close to every detail, and never let a build slip into a "production" mode. If your dream home is bigger than 2,000 sq/ft, we'll happily refer you to a larger builder we trust.
— 02 How is the price determined?
After a free consultation and lot review, we provide a detailed line-item quote based on your floor plan, finish selections, and site conditions. The quote is fixed-price wherever possible, with clearly identified allowances for items still to be selected (lighting, tile, hardware, etc.).
— 03 Do you handle permits and drawings?
Yes — every BZM build includes architectural drawings, engineering, and full City of Edmonton permitting. You'll never need to chase paperwork at City Hall.
— 04 What's the deposit structure?
Builds are typically structured as a series of progress draws tied to construction milestones (foundation, framing, lock-up, drywall, finishes, completion). The exact schedule is spelled out in your contract before any money changes hands.
— 05 What warranty do you offer?
Every BZM build comes with a written workmanship warranty covering labour and installation. Specific coverage and duration are detailed in your contract — and we stand behind it. If something isn't right, we come back.
— 06 Where do you build?
Greater Edmonton and surrounding communities — including St. Albert, Sherwood Park, Leduc, Spruce Grove, and the Edmonton metro area. If you're outside that range, ask us anyway — we'll let you know honestly whether it fits.
