01 —Our Story

Building right,
at a human scale.

BZM Enterprise was founded on a simple idea: that a small home, a kitchen reno, or a tenant fit-up deserves the same care, communication, and craft as a custom mansion. Most contractors chase scale — we chase quality.

We focus on builds up to 2,000 sq/ft because that's where we can stay close to every detail. From the first walkthrough to the final inspection, the same team is on the job — answering your calls, owning the timeline, and making sure the finished space is one you're proud to live in or open the doors to.

Over the years we've worked across Edmonton and the surrounding communities — infill builds, garden suites, full home renovations, and commercial leasehold improvements. Every project is different. The standard isn't.

02 —How We Work

Four steps,
no surprises.

Every BZM project follows the same simple flow — so you always know what's happening, what's next, and what it'll cost. No mystery line items, no missed deadlines.

01

Listen

We start with a free conversation — your space, your goals, your timeline, your budget. No pressure, no commitment.

02

Plan

A clear written scope and an honest quote. Drawings, finishes, milestones — everything spelled out before a hammer swings.

03

Build

Clean job sites, weekly updates, and the same crew you met on day one. Change orders are written and approved — never assumed.

04

Hand Off

Final walkthrough, deficiency list, and a workmanship warranty in writing. We don't disappear after the keys turn over.

03 —What We Stand For

Five things we
won't compromise on.

— 01

Honest scope.

If we can't do it well, we'll tell you. If a feature isn't worth the money, we'll say so. Our quotes are line-itemed, plain-language, and yours to keep.

— 02

Clean job sites.

Tools put away, dust controlled, debris hauled at the end of every day. Whether you're living in the house or running a business next door — you'll barely notice we're there.

— 03

One point of contact.

You'll always know who to call. The person who quoted your project is the person managing it — no handoffs, no telephone games.

— 04

Schedules that hold.

A start date that means start. A finish date we plan to beat. We don't pile up jobs — we close them out.

— 05

Warranties in writing.

Every BZM build and renovation comes with a written workmanship warranty. If something's not right, we come back. Simple as that.

04 —Our Team

The same crew,
start to finish.

Behind every BZM project is a small, tight crew — tradespeople we've worked with for years, who care about the finished product as much as we do. No subcontractor shuffling, no rotating faces, no excuses.

The result: a build that's coordinated, communicated, and consistent — exactly what you'd expect from a team that takes its name seriously.
